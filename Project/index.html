About us

Choosing Michael R. Spano & Co

as your Customs broker is a decision that elevates your international trade operations to the next level. Based in Franklin Square, New York, we bring decades of unmatched experience and a deep understanding of customs regulations, ensuring that your air, land, and sea freight moves seamlessly across borders. But we’re more than just a Customs broker—we’re experts in import consultants and freight forwarding, orchestrating the entire logistics chain with precision. Our commitment to personalized service means that every detail of your shipment is meticulously handled, minimizing delays, reducing costs, and providing you with peace of mind. We are also CTPAT certified, underscoring our commitment to secure and efficient supply chain practices. Whether navigating routine imports or managing complex international logistics, we provide the expertise, reliability, and forward-thinking solutions you need to stay ahead in a competitive global market.

Services

Customs Brokerage

Streamline your international trade with our expert customs brokerage services. We handle all customs documentation and procedures, ensuring your goods clear borders smoothly and compliantly, saving you time and avoiding costly delays

Import Consulting

Optimize your global trade strategy with our import consulting services. We provide expert guidance on import regulations, tariff classifications, and compliance, helping you navigate complex requirements and maximize efficiency in your supply chain

Freight Forwarding

Ensure your shipments arrive on time with our comprehensive freight forwarding services. We manage the entire logistics process, coordinating air, land, and sea transport to deliver your goods efficiently and cost-effectively, no matter where they’re headed
